Here I studied as a BSIT

I graduated in Informatics Northgate Alabang. Tuition is affordable. The school itself is very small. But the quality of the classroom and school itself is very nice. There are a few teachers who speak strictly English but most teachers speak Taglish. You can be an Informatics graduate and beat any student graduating from UP, DLSU, or Ateneo if you can prove you have top-notch English skills and have a lot of work experience. Informatics isn`t the best but it does offer great quality learning environment and very supportive teachers. If you want to graduate and get a job after graduation, here`s a tip don`t blame the school for you not getting a job. Blame yourself. You have to ask for help or look for jobs yourself. Like I said, a degree proves determination and perseverance.
